Fitz Roy, Patagonia Argentina - March 2019

Patagonia was one of the places I wanted to visit since I started climbing at the age of 14. The silouette of the mountains in this picture is used for "Patagonia" logo.

The chief, Squamish B.C. Canada - September 2018

Squamish is a popurar destination for climbers during the summer season. I stayed there for 3 weeks in September and climbed a wall called 'Chief' which is 600 m / 2,000 ft high granite wall.

El Capitan, Yosemite National Park, U.S.A. - June 2018

El Capitan is a 900 m / 3,000 ft high sheer granite wall. Climbing El Capitan is a dream for many climbers all over the world. I spent 3 nights on the wall to climb this with my friend.

While I was climbing this wall, I dropped my iphone from 600m / 2,000 ft high by accident...fortunately it did not hit anyone below.

Railay beach, Thaidand - March 2018

Railay beach is a paradise for climbers. I stayed there for 2 weeks with my wife and kids. It was my third time to visit Railay.

It was in 1996 that I visited this place for the fist time. Things changed a lot since then but the quality of the rocks remains great!
